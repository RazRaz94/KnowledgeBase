These are changes since Bel Ba’s shadow WR. Keep in mind that Drive refills may vary depends on how you end certain fights. This route also relies on completeing certain Test of Valor’s in Gold Medal.

  • Red = Important pick up / easy to forget
  • Orange = Important Purchase
  • Green = Equip, listed in suggested order for menu optimization
  • Purple = Optional Pick
