I. Preface

These are my notes and FAQs about the run and are subject to change at any time. Can apply same route as Oni difficulty. Though, strategies will vary because it require more passive approaches. i.e H.Splitter loop against Tenkai is not reliable. It's better to opt for Magic Crit > H.Splitter instead

Currently, the route got completely changed with the Test of Valor trigger skips. This means the shopping and some other room strategies will be changed completely. I will update this when I return to grinding it again.

Test of Valor playlist

  • 4: Rewards you Black Sword - 黒斬刀
  • 7: Rewards you Gleam which is very helpful in Test of Valor 13 and 16.
  • 10: Most important reward for Horns of Susano
  • 13: Rewards you Sky Sword that helps Soki to maintain the required DPS.
  • 18: Rewards you Roberto's ideal weapon for the boss rush section in Chapter 17
  • 19: Rewards you Tenkai's ideal weapon for the boss rush section in Chapter 17
  • Whenever you switch equipment during the menu always press START for quick exit.
  • On any movement section, camera changing = changes stick direction.
  • Most of your time saves comes from optimizing character switch.
  • Refer to the Menu optimization when it comes to buying and upgrading skills and such.

VI. FAQs

Is the run that long ?

Yes, not only due to the loads but also due to the disc switch we had to do in the mid-run.

How do you move fast ?
  • Flick left stick to the direction that you want while tapping guard button at the same time. Soki is the most controlled character because of his recovery from it and travel distance.
  • Reberto is the second best but the way his wave dash works is different you hold guard and press X for him to dash. To cancel his recover you flick left at any direction before pressing X again.
  • The rest of the characters works the same way as Soki but Ohatsu and Tenkai animation is too long which is why we use stance movement instead by flicking the left stick repeatedly while holding the Lock-On.
Why do you press then d-pad up before doing partner critical/issen ?

Because for some reason it gives you extra damage.

Why did my or partner critical/issen failed even after I got the timing right ?
  • Pressed to fast. This affects partner chain the most.
  • Not within the range
  • Recovering from Guard Block
  • Recovering from Guard Animation
  • Some enemies critical only works if you got hit. For Example, Mecha Heidyoshi
Why do you play JP release ?
  • Because enemies has higher defense in the US and EU release. Meaning, you will have to pick Fudo Horn in chapter 15 and before the final chapter, you need to upgrade Ohatsu's armor to lv10 so she can equip 2 Horn of Susano. This will make it possible to one-turn kill the final boss. At the moment, the speed difference between West and JP seems to be by 10m (NA/EU is yet to be optimized). This isn't really bad considering some PS2 game release can go waaay too much with the changes. Such as Chaos Legion with ridiculous upgrade costs or Devil May Cry 3 Vanilla lack of checkpoints. Luckily, Dawn of Dreams is more focused on character vs enemy scaling with each difficulties..
  • Another reason is because shipping from the west is simply too expensive for me so, I can only afford to buy Asian products from where I live.
  • Fortunately, this game has system language change to English. Although, I will still have JP text in case if I find texts in JP is simply faster.

Ohatsu is that busted ?

Yes, her shooting cancel is what makes her broken with overdrive or rapid fire. You can hold absorb soul then mash basic attack. In overdrive you can cancel her special attack with basic attack.

Why pick Jubei more often ?
  • Her Special Order lures and distracts enemy away.
  • Her weapons such as Muramasa and Masamune provides huge boost on Critical Damage (+40%).
How is chapter 13's painting puzzle being RNG ?
  • The first puzzle the paintings are either going to be in the even or odd buildings. To solve this as fast as possible, check building 1 first then if its not there you know it will be in 2 and 4. Therefore you can look for 2 then guess the 3rd number..
  • The Second puzzle you are just looking for the whale elephants or bear paintings corresponding to the building number. For example, if whale is in building 6 then the first number is 6. As a result, you can find 2 and guess the 3rd..
Difference between each critical/issen ?
  • Attack before an enemy attack - This is the true critical/issen and will reward you with the most souls and damage out of all methods. Naturally, the timing varies depending on the enemy. Sometimes you can initiate right before the enemy attack connects, and sometimes you have to attack a bit early and so on.
  • Guard break - The Kick will break the enemy's guard, allowing you to perform a critical/issen while they stagger.
  • Deflect Attack right after deflecting an attack - While the enemy is staggering from the deflect (L1 right before an attack) press the attack button to perform a critical/issen..
  • Oni Magic - (Use Oni Magic △ then attack ▢ while the enemy staggers): The Oni Magic will stun the enemy and allow you to perform a critical/issen. Keep in mind that the enemy must survive the Oni Magic hit in order to hit them with a critical/issen. Although, this can't be done with ice elemental magic.
