History

  • 2018 Joined Speedrun.com
  • 2019 Held Record DMC2 for a year.
  • 2020 Broke a long record holder for Castlevania: Lament of Innocence by 19s (34:39)
  • 2021 Attempted to branch out of action games by getting better at 2D platforms such as Rondo of Blood
  • 2022 Started the largest community project for the DMC community and made apperance in the GDQ event
  • 2023 Two times DMC3 Record and first to reach 57 RTA
  • 2025 Started to invest in High Diffculty content runs

These are just the main highlights

There are plenty of more that I have done through out the year. However, these are the ones that I feel proud of or find it interesting to share. At one point, there is a high chance where I might make another catalogs of completing diffcult challenges. I will add my marathon apperances later once I sort them out in my back up YT channel.

I am in a point where life got caught up with more responsibilities. Like it's really diffcult to me to run Ninja Gaiden Black more often now.

I hope that one day, the things that I spent time working with this website and archiving stuff that I find significant would help a lot of people preservation of this hobby and the games that I love.
